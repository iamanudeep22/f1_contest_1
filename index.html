Discover Mysuru

Mysuru is a city in the state of Karnataka, India. It is known as the cultural capital of Karnataka. Mysuru was the capital of the Wodeyar kings who ruled over the Mysore(now Mysuru) Kingdom for many centuries. Wodeyars were great patrons of art and music and have contributed significantly to make Mysuru a cultural centre. Mysuru is well known for its palaces, museums and art galleries and the festivities that take place here during the period of Dasara attract a worldwide audience. Mysuru has also lent its name to popular dishes like Mysore Masala Dosa and Mysore Pak. Mysuru is also the origin of the popular silk sari known as Mysore silk sari and has also given rise to a popular form of painting known as Mysore painting.
